## Immigration Court Clarification

The **Buffalo Immigration Court** is at **130 Delaware Avenue, Suite 300** — a separate building from 250 Delaware. It is an EOIR (DOJ) court, not ICE/DHS.

- The court building (referenced as 130 or 138 Delaware) is owned by Boyd Waterson, earning ~$2M/yr from EOIR
- ICE agents are *dispatched from* 250 Delaware to conduct arrests at the court
- The Velazquez courthouse arrest (May 2025) happened at 130 Delaware, not inside 250 Delaware
